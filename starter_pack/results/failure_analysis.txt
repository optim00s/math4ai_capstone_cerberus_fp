Failure-Case Analysis: Under-Capacity NN (h=2) on 10-Class Digits
============================================================

NN h=2  | Test Acc: 0.5897 | Test CE: 1.1133

With only 2 hidden units the network creates a 2-D bottleneck:
h = tanh(W1 x + b1) maps 64-D input to 2 dimensions, which is
insufficient to separate 10 classes.  Both train and val loss
remain high and accuracy plateaus far below what h=32 achieves.
